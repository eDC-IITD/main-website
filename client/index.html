About Us
The Entrepreneurship Development Cell of IIT Delhi was set up in January 2007 with a vision to promote and nurture Entrepreneurship amongst the student community and help aspiring student entrepreneurs achive their entrepreneurial aspirations.
Our Flagship Events

BECON

eDC IIT Delhi's annual Business & Entrepreneurship Conclave with 50+ events spread across 3 seeing 50000+ Footfall making it North India's biggest E-summit with PAN India reach and a very renowned guest list.

Ideathon 2022

eDC IIT Delhi is hosting its pitching competition 'IDEATHON 2022', where you can pitch your ideas to us and if your idea is selected as a winner, you will receive upto 1 lakh INR to help you expand your idea.

Mad For Ad

eDC-IIT Delhi with tech partner Metaschool is here with its first inter Hostel competition - 'Mad for Ad' where teams from all the hostels will battle out to create the best ads for the companies assigned to them.
